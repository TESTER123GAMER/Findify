Help & Guide

Getting Started

1. Choose a username generation mode from the dropdown
2. Set your preferred length and options
3. Click "Start Checking" to begin finding available usernames

Generation Modes

Random: Generates completely random usernames
Coherent: Creates themed usernames based on style
Themed: Uses specific word themes (Jesus, Emo, Love, etc.)
Custom: Create your own theme with custom words

Features

Q&A Forum: Ask questions and get help from the community
Groups: Join groups to compete and share progress
Achievements: Unlock achievements as you use the tool
Statistics: Track your progress and success rates

Data Saving

Auto-Save: Your progress is automatically saved
Export: Export your data to backup files
Import: Import previous data from backup files
Data Manager: Manage all your saved data

Tips

• Shorter usernames are more likely to be taken
• Try different themes and modes for variety
• Join groups to learn from experienced users
• Check the forum for community tips and tricks
